Chase & Status

Chase & Status are an English electronic music production duo composed of Saul Milton and Will Kennard. In addition, MC Rage and Andy Gangadeen also make up the live band. The duo are from London, and formed in 2003 after meeting at university in Manchester. The duo have since released four studio albums as well as collaborating with major artists such as Plan B, CeeLo Green, Rihanna, Example, and Tinie Tempah.

Bou

Bou is a drum and bass producer from the UK. He has been making waves in the drum and bass scene with his unique sound and high-energy tracks. Bou has released music on labels such as Critical Music, Souped Up Records, and Hospital Records. He has also collaborated with artists such as Serum, Simula, and Upgrade.

Track

The track "Baddadan" is a collaboration between Chase & Status and Bou. It features vocals from IRAH, Flowdan, Trigga, and Takura. The track is a high-energy drum and bass tune with a catchy hook and hard-hitting bassline. It has been a hit in the drum and bass scene and has been played at festivals and clubs around the world.
